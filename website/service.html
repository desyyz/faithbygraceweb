Customer Service & Support

We are here to help! Find answers to your questions about shipping, returns, sizing, and more.

Shipping & Delivery

**Processing Time:** Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days.

**Shipping Options:** We offer Standard (3-7 days) and Expedited (2-3 days) shipping within the US. International shipping times vary.

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped.

Returns & Exchanges

**Our Policy:** We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

**How to Return:** Please visit our [Returns Portal] or contact us at support@soullinen.com to initiate a return.

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where does the donation portion of my purchase go?

A: A portion of every sale supports a rotating list of vetted faith-based charities and non-profit organizations around the world. Details on our current partners can be found on our About page.

Q: How do I know what size to order?

A: We provide a detailed sizing chart on each product page. Our apparel generally runs true to size, but if you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

Q: Can I change or cancel my order?

A: We process orders quickly, but if you contact us within 2 hours of placing your order, we will do our best to accommodate changes or cancellations.
